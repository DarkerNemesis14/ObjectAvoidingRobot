The following the connection details of different components with the Arduino Uno:

## Sonar Sensor:
----------------
* VCC Pin: 2
* Trig Pin: 3
* Echo Pin: 4

## L298N Motor Controller :

----------------
* Input1 Pin: 8
* Input2 Pin: 9
* Input3 Pin: 10
* Input4 Pin: 11

## Servo Motor:

----------------
* Signal Pin: 5
